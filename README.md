AI should not be used lightly. It is a powerful tool that is able to process data incredibly fast, and complete tasks just as fast. It's critical to understand what it is you are prompting and tasking the AI to do, and it's effects on others. Beyond safety, it is also important to utilize AI effectively. The ease of AI has allowed for creations of very low-quality and even broken applications and features. It is important to understand what the AI generates, especially if what it's generating will be utilized by others. Applications have had security and privacy concerns due to the failure of verifying features generated by AI.
